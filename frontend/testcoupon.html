Get answers to your American Eagle FAQs before you shop

Does American Eagle have coupons?

Yes, with an American Eagle coupon, you can get big savings on apparel and accessories. A few examples we’ve seen were 30% off sitewide, 20% off in-store, and an extra 10% off all sale styles.

How do I get an American Eagle birthday discount?

Rewards members get an annual American Eagle discount code for 15% off, 20% off, or 25% off, depending on your rewards tier. Check your inbox near your birthday for your discount.

How do I use an American Eagle promo code?

Applying an American Eagle promo code at checkout is easy. Add products to your shopping bag, then click the shopping bag icon to begin checking out. On this page, find the “Apply a Promo Code” button under the “Offers” section. Click it, enter it, and click “Apply” to enjoy your savings.

When does American Eagle usually have special sales?

We’ve seen sales like 40% off sweaters, flannels, and fleece for Black Friday, up to 70% off during the End of Year Sale, and up to 25% off sweaters and sweatshirts during the Fall Sale. Check this page for limited-time deals, and maximize your savings.

How can I get an AE promo code for 25% off?

Get a 25% Off AE promo code when you refer a friend. To begin referring friends, go online and find the box that says “Want an Extra 25% Off?” Click it to sign up and start referring friends right away.

Does American Eagle have free shipping?

Yes, Real Rewards members get free shipping when they spend $75 and more. Add $75 or more to your cart and choose standard shipping at checkout, and you’ll automatically get your savings.

Save an extra buck at American Eagle Outfitters

Real savings for Real Rewards members

The Real Rewards program lets you earn points on every purchase. You can redeem 1250 points for a $5 reward code. When you first join, you’ll be a Level 1 member. That gets you 10 points per $1 spent, a 15% coupon on your birthday, double points on jeans, and access to Aerie Days and members-only sale events.

Spend $150/year to get Level two status. You’ll get all the above perks plus 15 points per $1 spent, free shipping on orders over $50, and a 20% off birthday coupon. When you spend $350/year, you’ll get Level three status. You’ll get 20 points per $1 spent, free shipping with no minimums, free returns on all orders, and a 25% off birthday coupon.

Score a 30% American Eagle promo code with the credit card

When you first sign up for the American Eagle credit card, you’ll get a promo code for 30% off any purchase. You’ll also get lots of other perks, including double points on everything, including 4x points on jeans.

You’ll also get a “Pick Your Own Sale Day,” free shipping and returns on all orders, and access to exclusive cardmember sales. These perks also stack with Real Rewards membership benefits for additional savings. You’re not limited to shopping at American Eagle, either. With the credit card, you can also get five points per $1 spent everywhere VISA credit cards are accepted. American Eagle promo code, extra perks, and rewards points? What are you waiting for?
